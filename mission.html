Mission

At English Tea Shop, we work closely with everyone in our value chain to make our planet a better place for all living beings. We want to be part of creating a world that’s both environmentally secure and economically prosperous for generations to come. A world where all people are treated fairly and given the chance to grow and develop.Our mission is to create the finest organic teas while having a net positive impact on the planet and the lives of everyone involved from farm to cup.
